📄 Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Product Name: CRM application
🧠 Product Overview
Product Type: Web & Mobile Application
Platform: React (Vite) + Supabase
Target Users:
Freelancers
Small Business Owners
Agencies (Design, Marketing, IT)
Goal:
To help users manage customer data, track leads, and increase sales through a simple, centralized, and affordable CRM system.
❗ Problem Statement
Users currently face the following challenges:
Customer data is scattered across platforms (WhatsApp, Excel, Notes)
Missed follow-ups lead to lost deals
No clear visibility of lead status
Poor team coordination
Difficult sales tracking and reporting
Solution:
A centralized CRM platform that:
Organizes customer data
Automates follow-ups
Simplifies sales tracking

3.1 Contact Management

Log calls, meetings, and messages

Converted leads
